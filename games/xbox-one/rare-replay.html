Rare Replay is a celebrated compilation for the Xbox One, developed by Rare and published by Microsoft Studios in 2015. This remarkable collection brings together 30 iconic titles from Rare’s storied past, ranging from beloved classics like Banjo-Kazooie and Battletoads to groundbreaking adventures such as Perfect Dark. Each game has been thoughtfully updated for modern play while preserving its original charm, and players can dive deeper with unlockable behind-the-scenes commentary and historical insights. Rare Replay not only offers a nostalgic journey through gaming history but also showcases the innovative spirit that has defined Rare as one of the industry's most influential studios.
